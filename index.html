About WASSCE Ace

WASSCE Ace was born from a simple yet powerful mission: to provide every West African Senior School Certificate Examination (WASSCE) candidate in Sierra Leone with the tools they need to excel. We believe that exam success should not be limited by access to expensive tutoring or physical textbooks, but should be available to every student with a desire to learn.

Our app is a comprehensive study partner designed to help you prepare effectively and confidently. We offer a vast library of real past questions, detailed study notes, and engaging video lessons created by expert educators who understand the curriculum inside and out.

We operate on a 'freemium' model. Students can access a significant amount of content for free, including practice questions and select video lessons. For those who want to unlock the full potential of their study, our Premium Bundle provides unlimited access to all past questions, complete answer keys, all video lessons, and exclusive study guides. This model allows us to keep the core app accessible to everyone while funding continuous development and content creation.

A key goal of WASSCE Ace is to combat the inefficient and often unreliable spread of exam answers on social media. By providing a centralized, trusted platform with verified solutions and clear explanations, we offer a structured and more effective way to learn from past papers. Our platform promotes genuine understanding over simple memorization.

Need help or have a question? You can chat with our admin team directly through the app at your convenience. We are here to support you on your journey to success. With WASSCE Ace, you're not just studying—you're preparing to ace your exams with confidence.

Privacy Policy

Last Updated: 13 September 2025

WASSCE Ace ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our application and visit our website.

1. Information We Collect

We may collect information about you in a variety of ways. The information we may collect includes:

  • Usage Data: We automatically collect anonymous usage information, such as which features of the app you interact with, to help us improve our services.
  • Anonymous Analytics: We use services like Firebase Analytics to collect anonymous data, including your device type and operating system, to understand how our app is being used and to enhance user experience.
  • User IDs: To manage premium subscriptions and user progress, we assign an anonymous, randomly generated user ID. We do not link this ID to any personally identifiable information like your name or email unless you voluntarily provide it (e.g., through customer support).

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Provide, operate, and maintain our application.
  • Improve, personalize, and expand our services.
  • Understand and analyze how you use our app.
  • Process your transactions for premium services.
  • Communicate with you for customer service and support.

3. Data Security

We use administrative, technical, and physical security measures to help protect your information. All data is stored on secure servers provided by Google Firebase. While we have taken reasonable steps to secure the information you provide to us, please be aware that no security measures are perfect or impenetrable.

4. Third-Party Services

We do not sell, trade, or otherwise transfer your personally identifiable information to outside parties. All data processing is handled through trusted platforms like Google Firebase and Google Play Store for payment processing.

5. Contact Us

If you have questions or comments about this Privacy Policy, please contact us through the admin chat feature in the app.
